Silky smooth and perfectly balanced, our latte is a coffee lover's dream. Made with a single shot of our premium espresso and generously steamed milk, creating a creamy texture with just a thin layer of microfoam. The higher milk-to-coffee ratio makes this drink milder and sweeter than a cappuccino, while still maintaining the rich espresso flavor. Perfect for those who enjoy a smooth, comforting coffee experience with beautiful latte art on top.
